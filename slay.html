About

Blending analytics, design, and storytelling

I thrive where data, people, and systems meet — using quantitative tools and human-centered thinking to make complex decisions feel intuitive and fair.

At CU Boulder, I’ve shaped my coursework and projects around three through-lines: rigorous analytics, inclusive digital design, and creative communication.

In my internship with United Airlines’ Pilot Workforce Planning & Strategy team, I worked with large operational datasets and network constraints to help support staffing and scheduling decisions. That experience grounded my interest in aviation strategy and showed me how data impacts crews, passengers, and the business — not just spreadsheets.

On campus, I bring that same mindset to the Cultural Events Board (CEB) and CU Abhinaya, where I work on everything from outreach and marketing to websites, choreography, and event logistics. I care deeply about digital equity: making sure the sites, tools, and experiences we build are accessible across devices, bandwidth, and abilities.

Looking ahead, I’m excited by roles where I can help teams ask better questions with data while designing experiences that people actually enjoy using.
