About Us

CU Abhinaya is the University of Colorado Boulderâ€™s premier Indian classical dance team, dedicated to sharing the elegance, storytelling, and cultural richness of South Asian traditions. Rooted in classical styles such as Bharatanatyam, Kathak, and Kuchupudi, our performances honor centuries of artistry while also embracing the creativity of contemporary movement. By blending tradition with new music, we bring a modern twist to classical dance that resonates with both South Asian communities and wider audiences. Our team performs at a variety of cultural showcases and community events throughout CU Boulder and the greater Denver area. Each performance is an opportunity to foster cultural appreciation, create connections, and share the joy of dance with diverse audiences. More than just a performance group, CU Abhinaya is a community. We are committed to providing students of all backgrounds an inclusive space to explore Indian classical dance, grow as artists, and build lasting friendships. Whether through high-energy group numbers, expressive solos, or collaborative events, we aim to highlight the power of dance as both an art form and a cultural bridge.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide students of all backgrounds an opportunity to explore, learn, and express themselves through the art of classical Indian dance, while fostering cultural appreciation, inclusivity, and a strong sense of community.
